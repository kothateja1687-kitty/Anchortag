    1. Crop Farming:

      Growing plants for food or commercial use. Examples:

      • Rice
      • Wheat
      • Maize
      • Sugarcane
      • Cotton
      • Vegetables
      • Fruits

    2. Animal Farming (Livestock);

      Raising animals for meat, milk, eggs, wool. Examples:

      • Cows
      • Sheep
      • Goats
      • Poultry (chicken
      • Buffalo

    3. Dairy Farming;

      Producing milk and milk products.

    4. Organic Farming;

      Using natural fertilizers and avoiding chemicals.

    5. Mixed Farming;

      Both crop growing + animal rearing together.

    6. Aquaculture;

      Fish farming.

  2. Factors Needed for Farming:

    1. Soil;

      Good fertile soil like black soil, alluvial soil.

    2. Water;

      Sources: Rain, rivers, wells, canals.

    3. Seeds;

      Certified, Disease-free, Suitable for the soil & climate

    4. Fertilizers & Manure;

      Chemical fertilizers (NPK), Organic manure, Compost, Vermicompost

    5. Labour;

      Sowing, Weeding, Irrigation, Harvesting, Transportation

    6. Capital (Money);

      Seeds, Fertilizers, Pesticides, Machinery, Water, Labour,
